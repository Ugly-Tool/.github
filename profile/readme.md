**ugly // tool** wraps your existing Gradio, Streamlit, or HF Space in a polished, research-grade presentation layer. No code ingestion. No redesign. Just describe your tool, pick a design system, and download something that doesn't look like a science fair project.
 
Your backend stays yours. We're flattered, but we'll leave the touching to you.
 
---
 
### Design systems
 
**[Vellum](#)** — warm stone · serif · research-grade · for the analyst who wants their tool to look like it belongs in a journal
 
**[After Hours](#)** — dark charcoal · amber · geometric sans · for the builder who works late and ships serious things
 
**[Clean Room](#)** — crisp white · clinical blue · precision sans · for the tool that needs to look controlled and trustworthy
 
---
 
### How it works
 
**01 · Describe it** — tell us what your tool does, what goes in, what comes out. Or show us via url.
 
**02 · Pick a system** — three design systems. Fixed palettes. Trust us. We've seen your work.
 
**03 · Download** — A look for your tool — two clean files (an HTML shell and a CSS theme) in one of our three finished looks: After Hours, Vellum, Clean Room. Use the HTML as a standalone front-facing page, drop the CSS into your existing app, or both. Your logic stays yours.
 
---
 
### What we don't do
 
We do not ingest your code. We do not store anything after generation. We do not redesign your tool. We generate a presentation layer from your description or url. Everything else is between you and your IDE.
