**ugly // tool** wraps your existing Gradio, Streamlit, or HF Space in a polished, research-grade presentation layer. No code ingestion. No redesign. Just describe your tool, pick a design system, and download something that doesn't look like a science fair project.
 
We're flattered, but we'll leave the touching to you.

### Design systems
 
**[Vellum](#)** — warm stone · serif · research-grade · for the analyst who wants their tool to look like it belongs in a journal
 
**[After Hours](#)** — dark charcoal · amber · geometric sans · for the builder who works late and ships serious things
 
**[Clean Room](#)** — crisp white · clinical blue · precision sans · for the tool that needs to look controlled and trustworthy
 
---
 
### How it works
 
**01 · Describe it** — tell us what your tool does, what goes in, what comes out. Or show us. Upload a screenshot labeled exactly as you'd expect.
 
**02 · Pick a system** — three opinionated design systems. Fixed palettes. Trust us. We've seen your work.
 
**03 · Download** — polished static HTML wrapper, documentation block, branded header and footer. Your logic stays with you.
 
---
 
### What we don't do
 
We do not ingest your code. We do not store your screenshots after generation. We do not redesign your tool. We generate a presentation layer from your description. Everything else is between you and your IDE.
